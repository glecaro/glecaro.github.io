Personal Site

Hi! I am a Research Associate at Innovations for Poverty Action working in gender, financial Inclusion and machine Learning. My research interests lie at the intersection between applied microeconomics and development economics. I am currently working in this project using machine learning and big data to provide access to microcredit for low-income women without credit histories.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Economics from the University of Manchester and a Master's degree in Public Policy from University College London.
